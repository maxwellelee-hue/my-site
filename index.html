OUR METHODS

Cinch traps.
Five visits.
Zero shortcuts.

We use cinch traps — fast, lethal, and ecologically sound. Every job begins with a full property walk to map the infestation, followed by four return visits to monitor, reset, and confirm the gophers are gone for good.

  1. 01

    Property walk & assessment

    We walk the full site with you, identify active tunnel systems, understand your priorities, and form a plan before a single trap goes in.

  2. 02

    Cinch trap placement

    Traps are set directly inside active tunnels — the most effective placement method. Cinch traps kill quickly and leave no chemicals in the soil.

  3. 03 – 05

    Four follow-up visits

    We return to monitor activity, reset traps where needed, and verify the infestation is fully eliminated before we sign off on the job.
